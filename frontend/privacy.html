Privacy Policy

Job Apply — an AI job-application assistant operated by Corey Laverdiere.

What we collect

  • Account info: email address and password hash (or Google account identifier if you sign in with Google).
  • Content you provide: your resume, profile/voice guide, job postings you paste in, and generated application materials (resumes, cover letters, interview prep, thank-you emails).
  • Application tracker data: companies, roles, statuses, notes, and calendar events you create.
  • If you connect Slack or Microsoft Teams: your workspace/tenant identity and messages sent to the bot, used only to link your bot identity to your Job Apply account and process the commands you run.

How we use it

  • To generate tailored resumes, cover letters, and interview prep using the Anthropic Claude API.
  • To store your files in Google Drive (if you connect it) and in our storage backend (Tigris S3), so you can access and re-download your materials.
  • To send transactional email (verification, password reset, and optional reminder/digest notifications you opt into) via Resend.
  • To operate the application tracker and calendar features you use.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data.
  • We don't use your resume or application content to train models.
  • We don't share your data with third parties except the service providers above, solely to operate the app on your behalf.

Your controls

  • You can update or delete your profile, resume, and tracked applications at any time from your account.
  • You can unlink Slack/Teams at any time (/unlink), and disable any email notification from your Profile settings.
  • To request full account deletion, email cdl825@gmail.com.
